Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web development service?

Our web development service includes custom design, responsive development, content integration, basic SEO setup, and deployment. We also provide training on how to manage your website and offer ongoing support options.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A basic website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects like e-commerce sites or custom web applications may take 8-12 weeks or longer. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

Yes, we offer managed hosting solutions optimized for performance and security. We can also assist with domain registration and management. Alternatively, you're welcome to use your preferred hosting provider.

What if I need changes after the project is completed?

We offer various maintenance plans to handle updates and changes post-launch. For one-off changes, we bill at our standard hourly rate. All our projects include a 30-day warranty period for bug fixes at no additional cost.
